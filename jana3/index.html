Passenger Rights on an Airplane

Know your rights in case of flight delays or cancellations — clear, practical, and easy to follow.

1

Right to Information

The airline must inform passengers immediately and clearly of any delay or cancellation, including expected departure time and reasons.

2

Right to Assistance and Care

Passengers receive assistance depending on delay length:

After 1 hour: Refreshments and beverages.
After 3 hours: A convenient meal.
After 6 hours: Hotel accommodation and transport if necessary.
3

Right to Compensation

Compensation may be provided for long delays or cancellations. Amount depends on flight distance and delay duration according to regulations.

4

Right to Refund

If the delay exceeds two hours, passengers can request a full refund or choose alternative travel offered by the airline.

Note: Guidelines may vary based on local regulations and airline policies.
